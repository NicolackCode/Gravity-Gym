# Prompts Google Labs Flow (Vidéo & Image)

Voici les prompts ultra-détaillés conçus pour exploiter au mieux les capacités de Google Labs Flow (génération d'images et vidéos avec les modèles Imagen/Veo).
Ces assets viendront remplacer les placeholders CSS actuels pour donner au site le rendu "Premium Architectural" final.

## 📸 1. Section Hero (Vidéo d'arrière-plan ou Image Cinematic)

_L'objectif est d'avoir un visuel de fond extrêmement immersif, sombre et haut de gamme._

**Prompt Image (Pour générer une base) :**

> "A photorealistic, highly detailed wide-angle architectural interior shot of a futuristic premium gym. Brutalist architecture, raw concrete walls, matte black metal structures, and subtle warm tungsten lighting. In the exact center, a massive vertical glass and steel column housing a huge concrete cylinder (a gravity battery). High-end fitness machines are arranged circularly around the central column. Warm, moody, cinematic lighting, volumetric fog, dark and luxurious atmosphere, 8k resolution, architectural photography style."

**Prompt Vidéo (Veo - Si tu utilises l'image ci-dessus comme input de départ) :**

> "Cinematic slow continuous tracking shot moving forward into the premium gym. The massive concrete cylinder inside the central glass column is slowly, silently moving upwards in the background. The warm tungsten lights gently flicker as energy is produced. Extremely realistic, high-end architectural visualization, flawless lighting, 24fps."

## ⚙️ 2. Section La Pile à Gravité (Rendu 3D focus produit)

_On veut un gros plan technique et esthétique sur le mécanisme lui-même._

**Prompt Image :**

> "Extreme close-up macro photography of a massive industrial gravity battery mechanism. Thick matte black steel cables pulling a heavy raw concrete block. Brass and copper accents on the pulleys. High technical detail, mechanical aesthetic, sharp focus on the cables and the texture of the concrete. Moody dramatic lighting, dark background, premium industrial design, 8k, Unreal Engine 5 render style."

**Prompt Vidéo (Veo) :**

> "Slow motion close-up of thick industrial steel cables pulling upwards. The camera slowly tilts down to reveal the heavy concrete mass rising smoothly. Realistic tension, subtle dust particles in the air, cinematic moody lighting, hyper-detailed mechanical movement."

## 🌿 3. Section Impact / Ambiance Végétale (Pour accompagner les données)

_Pour illustrer la partie écologique sans tomber dans le cliché, on veut marier le brutale et le végétal._

**Prompt Image :**

> "Architectural shot of a premium eco-friendly gym interior. Raw concrete walls seamlessly integrated with massive lush green vertical living walls (plant walls). Matte black modern treadmills facing the greenery. Natural sunlight filtering through a skylight, creating beautiful soft shadows. Serene, luxurious, sustainable design, photorealistic, 8k."

**Prompt Vidéo (Veo) :**

> "Slow pan across a luxurious brutalist gym interior. The focus shifts from a modern matte black rowing machine to a stunning vertical living wall bathed in soft natural sunlight. Peaceful, premium, high-end architectural video."
